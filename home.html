About Naropil Children Foundation

Naropil Children Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to transforming the lives of vulnerable children. We believe that every child deserves a fair chance at life, regardless of their background.

Founded on the principle of compassion, our mission is to break the cycle of poverty through holistic interventions targeting education, health, and social welfare.

Our Mission

To empower disadvantaged children and youth to reach their full potential by providing access to quality education, healthcare, and psycho-social support.

Our Vision

A society where every child is empowered, educated, and equipped to become a responsible, self-reliant global citizen.
